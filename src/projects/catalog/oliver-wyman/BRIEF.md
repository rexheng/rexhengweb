# Oliver Wyman — sprite brief

**Project:** Oliver Wyman Datathon — 1st place. 14-variable linear
regression on marathon performance data.
**Sprite role:** sport × data prop. A running track silhouette with a
scatterplot trend line inside.

## Why this sprite

The project crosses endurance sport (marathon) with quantitative analysis
(regression). The stadium track reads as "marathon"; the ascending line of
dots inside reads as "regression". Together they are unambiguous.

## Silhouette targets

1. Oval track ring — outer 1.5×D × 0.9×D, tube radius 0.08×D.
2. Short pedestal beneath — radius 1.0×D, height 0.08×D.
3. 7 scatter dots inside the track, ascending from bottom-left to top-right,
   colour progressing from pale to saturated red.

## No ability

Static prop.
