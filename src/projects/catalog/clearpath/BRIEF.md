# ClearPath — sprite brief

**Project:** ClearPath — Claude Hackathon @ LSE.
**Sprite role:** service icon. An NHS-blue plus with a clock face on top,
reading as "NHS + time".

## Why this sprite

ClearPath is about reducing NHS cancer waiting times, so the sprite's two
readable elements are the NHS brand cross and a clock. Together they
shorthand the product's premise: the health service, the clock ticking,
and a path through it.

## Silhouette targets

1. NHS-blue cross slab — arms 1.2×D × 0.36×D × 0.24×D.
2. Clock disc on top — radius 0.30×D, 12 tick marks around the rim.

## No ability

Static prop. The cross+clock reads from any angle.
