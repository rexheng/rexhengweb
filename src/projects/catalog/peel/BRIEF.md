# Peel — sprite brief

**Project:** Peel — Agentic Society Hedera × LSE. Tokenised food-waste
marketplace.
**Sprite role:** market / sustainability prop. A stack of produce crates
with a coin stamped by a leaf — marketplace + food + token.

## Silhouette targets

1. Three slatted produce crates, stacked. Each 1.0×D × 0.35×D × 1.0×D.
2. A small gold coin on the top crate — radius 0.22×D, height 0.05×D.
3. A stretched green leaf shape on the coin's face — acts as the stamp.

## No ability

Static prop.
