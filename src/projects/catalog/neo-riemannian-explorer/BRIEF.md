# Neo-Riemannian Explorer — sprite brief

**Project:** Neo-Riemannian Explorer — interactive music theory visualisation.
**Card on projects/index.html:** https://neoriemannian.vercel.app/
**Sprite role:** prop. A recognisable mini piano keyboard that reads
instantly as "music tool" from across the ragdoll playground.

## Visual target

- A short octave of piano keys (one full octave, C to C): 7 white keys +
  5 black keys, laid out on a slim horizontal slab.
- Wide and flat — sprite is meant to sit on the ground like a desk toy.
- Accent is a muted purple (`#8a6bbf`) — hint at the harmonic-analysis
  mood without being loud.

No ability — music-theory apps don't map cleanly onto a physics gag.
Prop spawns, can be grabbed and kicked around. That's enough.
