# LSE Peter — sprite brief

**Project:** LSE Peter — educational Instagram (@lse.peter) for LSE students.
**Card on projects/index.html:** https://www.instagram.com/lse.peter
**Sprite role:** character. A stylised Peter avatar wearing the LSE
identity — purple body (LSE brand), book-stack emblem above the head.

## Visual target

- Standard Peter silhouette (shared primitive `peterAvatar`).
- LSE purple body (`#6b1f5f`).
- Emblem: a small stack of books above the head — reads "academic".
- No ability.
